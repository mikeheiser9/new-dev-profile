About Me

My name is Mike Heiser and I am a web developer living in downtown LA. I enjoy creating dynamic web applications and expressing myself creativly through my projects. I have experience in both web development and e-commerce. I enjoy working on e-commerce platforms such as Shoify and Magento and am a certified Shopify Expert. I also have experience with Wordpress. I can handle any size project from small changes to your existing website to a full e-commerce store or mobile app build. Please send me a message in the CONTACT section below!
